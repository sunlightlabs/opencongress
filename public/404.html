OpenCongress

OpenCongress allows anyone to follow legislation in Congress, from bill introduction to floor vote. Learn more about issues you care about and connect with others who share similar views. OpenCongress was founded by the Participatory Politics Foundation in 2006 and operated as a joint project with the Sunlight Foundation until May 2013.
